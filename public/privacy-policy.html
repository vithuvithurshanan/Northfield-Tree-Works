Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 22, 2026

Northfield Tree Works ("Northfield Tree Works," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website (the "Site") and how we use, share, and protect it, including information collected when you request a quote, contact us, or opt in to receive SMS/text message communications.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details you provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Details about your service request, including service type, property notes, and photos you choose to upload.
  • Your consent and preferences when you opt in to SMS/text messaging.
  • Basic usage data (such as pages visited) collected automatically to help us maintain and improve the Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to quote requests, schedule appointments, and provide the tree care services you request.
  • To send appointment confirmations, dispatch/arrival updates, and service-related notifications by phone, email, or SMS.
  • With your consent, to send promotional offers or updates about Northfield Tree Works.
  • To maintain business records and comply with legal or insurance obligations.
SMS / Text Messaging Data: Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Mobile information is used solely to deliver the services you requested and will not be sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties for their own marketing use.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only with:

  • Employees, subcontractors, or crew members directly involved in fulfilling your service request.
  • Service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., scheduling, payment processing, or SMS/CRM platforms), bound to protect your data.
  • Authorities where required by law, subpoena, or to protect the safety of our customers or staff.

4. SMS Terms Summary

By submitting your phone number through our contact form or otherwise opting in, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Northfield Tree Works related to your service request, appointment reminders, dispatch updates, and (if you separately opt in) promotional messages. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for assistance. See our Terms of Service for full SMS program details.

5. Data Retention & Security

We retain your information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

6. Your Choices

  • You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any text.
  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
